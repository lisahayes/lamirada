Room by room

Where leaks show up in a home

Leaks announce themselves differently depending on the room. A kitchen leak pools under the sink cabinet or behind the dishwasher. A bathroom one stains the ceiling below or softens the floor by the tub. The laundry hides a slow drip behind the washer hoses, the garage shows it at the water heater, and an attic line can travel down a wall two rooms away. Knowing how each room tends to leak shapes the walk through, so we follow the water back to its source instead of trusting where it landed. A ceiling stain in particular almost never sits directly below its source, which is exactly why we trace the path rather than cut where the mark shows.
