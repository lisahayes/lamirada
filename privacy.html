Privacy policy

This page explains what happens when you call or visit this site. It is written plainly and kept short on purpose.

This is a phone-first service. We do not run contact forms on this site, so we do not collect names, emails, or addresses through the web pages. When you call (562) 488-9614, your phone may show your number, and we use it only to return your call and arrange leak service.

Calls and call routing

Calls placed to the number on this site are routed to a licensed leak detection and repair specialist. Site analytics

Like most websites, this site may use standard analytics to count visits and see which pages are read. That data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. We do not use it to build a profile of you.

Sharing

We share your details only with the specialist who handles your leak, and only to get the work done. We do not sell your information to third parties.

Your choices

Updates
